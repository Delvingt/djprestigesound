Harmony, Rhythm, and Passion at the Heart of Your Wedding

HarmonYou orchestrates your wedding like a musical score: rhythmic, precise, and uniquely yours. Our family team brings together complementary talents: Quentin, musician; Jessica, specialist emcee; Vally, decorator and photographer; and Julien, logistics expert. Together, we create weddings that truly reflect you.

Harmonize Your Ceremony with Our Unique Offers:

"Animation Only" Package

Personalized emcee service by Jessica, with a custom script created from a questionnaire and a video conference interview. An emotional and authentic ceremony.

"Animation + Sound System" Package

In addition to Jessica's emcee service, enjoy a professional sound system installation with wireless microphone and integration of your selected tracks for the perfect ambiance.

"Animation + Sound System + Live Piano Musician" Package

Add a live piano performance by Quentin for a romantic and memorable ceremony, complementing the emcee and sound system services.
