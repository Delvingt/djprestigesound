The Ultimate Musical Experience for Your Event

At DJ Prestige Sound, we go beyond simple mixing to offer you an immersive live experience that will captivate your guests. Whether you desire the energy of a live funk band, the elegance of a jazzy cocktail with saxophone, or a wild night blending DJ and live performance, we have the ideal formula for you.

What The Funk - Cover Band

Awaken your body and mind with the powerful groove of What The Funk, driven by a duo of mesmerizing voices! Inspired by James Brown, The Meters, Earth, Wind & Fire, and featuring a repertoire focused on funk, soul, and disco, this 9-piece band will make you groove under any circumstance with an enchanting rhythm that will make you forget the monotony of the dancefloor for a feverish journey through the history of groove from the '60s to today. It’s time to pull out your bell-bottoms, your Ray-Bans, and your afro cuts! FreakOut! The Freak is chic!

DJ SAX – The Energy of the DJ, The Magic of the Sax

Immerse yourself in a unique musical experience where the power of an explosive DJ set meets the warmth and intensity of a live saxophone. DJ SAX is the perfect balance between electronic groove and the spontaneity of an acoustic instrument, creating an irresistible atmosphere at every event.

Whether it's for an elegant cocktail or an electrifying dance party, our saxophonists Ilia and Manon elevate every note to move your audience. From captivating melodic flights to electrifying solos, DJ SAX transforms your evening into a true live show, combining pounding beats with dynamic stage energy.
